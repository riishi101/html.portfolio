The Best Movies According to Rishi

My top 5 movies of all-time.

The Last Samurai

This movie is about a man who travels to Japan to fight for The Japanese Emperor against the Samurai. The Samurai opposed the idea of modernisation of the country. They were good people with different opinions.

The Hangover

This is one of the funniest movies that I have watched. Period. The movie is about four friends having a bachelor party in Las Vegas.

Tropic Thunder

Another great comedy movie, little bit out of touch nowadays but very funny movie.

Intersteller

Intersteller is a sci-fi movie and probabily the best sci-fi movie ever.

A Silent Voice

My favourite anime of all time. Great movie protraying the difficulty faced by teenagers in Japanese Society.
